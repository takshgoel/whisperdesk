What makes it different
Live mic transcription

Press record, start talking. Your words appear as you speak using Whisper AI running locally on your CPU.

Audio file support

Drop in any MP3, WAV, M4A or OGG file and get a full transcript in seconds. Drag-and-drop or browse.

Fully offline

Whisper runs entirely on your machine. No API calls, no internet required, no usage limits. Ever.

One-click export

Copy the full transcript to clipboard or save it as a .txt file with a timestamped filename instantly.

Model size selector

Choose between Tiny (fast), Base (balanced), or Small (accurate) depending on your hardware.

Zero cost

Free to download and use forever. No account, no subscription, no hidden charges.

How it works
01
Download & install

Run the installer on Windows 10 or 11. Python and ffmpeg are bundled — nothing else to configure.

02
First launch

On the first run, WhisperDesk downloads the Whisper model (~140MB). After that, it works completely offline.

03
Start transcribing

Hit record to capture your mic, or drop in an audio file. Your transcript appears in seconds, ready to copy or save.
